description: >
  Bring trinkets and collectables in good condition. This can include figurines,
  plushies, keychains, pins, random trinkets, stickers, and more. Please don't
  purchase anything for the swap and don't bring anything drop shipped (ex. 100
  plastic ducks or sticker multipacks from Amazon).


  There will be tables where you can set up your own stash to trade and well as
  tables where you can take and leave items. If you are using one of our tables,
  we may ask you to rotate out so others can have a turn. You can also bring
  your own table and set up outside if the weather is good. This event is only
  for swapping items, not selling items.


  We will also have a craft area with painting supplies to decorate ceramic or
  wooden pieces.
