description: >
  Start the new year off with a clothing swap!


  Bring **clean** and dry clothing in good condition and swap for new to you
  clothing. Please avoid bringing clothing with strong scents, excessive pet
  fur, or other allergens. Also, please only bring **one bag** of clothing. 


  If you are bringing clothing to swap, please bring it before 2:00 pm. After
  2:00 pm, we invite any community members or organizations to take as much as
  they will use. 


  Schedule:


  * 11:00 am - Clothing Swap begins

  * 12:00 pm - 2:00 pm - Mending Station

  * 2:00 pm - Take what you'll use


  Mending Station: Do you have clothing that needs to be mended? We'll have a
  mending station set up with experts who can help you troubleshoot how to mend
  your items. Or, if they can't be mended, we'll be collecting textile waste for
  American Textile Recycling Service.


  Waste Knot will have upcycled items for sale by donation.  Other local vendors
  will be attending as well!


  Waste Knot Item Donations accepted:


  * Plastic Produce Bag Netting

  * \#2 Plastic Bottle Tops (clean)

  * Thread and Sewing Materials


  Goblin Market Item Donations accepted: 


  * Blank t-shirts, tote bags, and sweatshirts

  * Tablecloths
