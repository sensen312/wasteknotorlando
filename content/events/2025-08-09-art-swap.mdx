  - title: FAQ
    questions:
      - question: How does it work?
        answer: >
          Bring arts and craft supplies that you are looking to swap. For most
          of the event, we ask that you swap items of equivalent value. There
          will also be a free section where you can take as much as you like.
      - question: What can I bring?
        answer: >
          Arts and Craft supplies in good condition. This can include supplies
          for painting, drawing, sculpting, sewing, yarn crafts, needlework,
          papercraft, jewelry making, and more.
      - question: Can I bring art I already made?
        answer: >
          Yes, there will be a designated area for you to trade your art with
          others. We cannot accept donations of completed art, so please be
          prepared to take home art that you were not able to trade. We also
          will be limiting how long each person can set up at a table, depending
          on demand.
      - question: Where should I park?
        answer: >
          There is parking at the church and street parking nearby. Follow all
          street signs and don't block anyone in.
      - question: How accessible is the event?
        answer: >
          There are no steps required for entry and there is a gender-neutral
          accessible bathroom. The parking lot is a combination of gravel and
          dirt.
      - question: Are there going to be food trucks?
        answer: >
          There will not be any food vendors at this event. There will be water
          available if you bring a reusable water bottle and there are many
          great local restaurants on Corrine Drive nearby.
      - question: Is the event free?
        answer: >
          Yes, but donations are gratefully accepted. Goblin Market and Waste
          Knot Orlando will have merch for sale, and Audubon Park Church will
          have their Hidden Treasures Boutique open for sales.
      - question: Is the event indoors?
        answer: >
          Yes, the event is completely indoors. The venue is air conditioned,
          but still be prepared for summer heat.
      - question: Can I volunteer?
        answer: >
          Yes, Email
          [wasteknotorlando@gmail.com](mailto\:wasteknotorlando@gmail.com) for
          details
