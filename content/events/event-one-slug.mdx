Make your own upcycled piece of art! Bryan (aka Recycled Horrors

Art) and Sarah (Waste Knot Orlando) will be leading a workshop about

making your own upcycled art. Provided supplies: Canvas, glue,

recycled materials (you can also bring some of your own) Suggested

donation: $10-$40 (sliding scale) TUA members can attend for free.
