  Don't be a craft supply dragon with a hoard of supplies you never use. Swap out arts and crafts supplies you aren't using for new-to-you supplies. 

  You can bring supplies for drawing, painting, sculpting, knitting, crocheting, sewing, embroidery, rug making, collaging, and more! Just put the supplies in the section they belong in and take other supplies that you'll use. 

  If you want to swap your own finished art, we'll have some designated areas set up. We are not accepting donations of completed art. 

  Mending Station: Do you have clothing that needs to be mended? We'll have a mending station set up from 11 am - 2 pm with experts who can help you troubleshoot how to mend your items. Limit of 1 item per person and we will have a sign up sheet at the event. 

  Waste Knot will have upcycled items for sale by donation.  Other local vendors will be attending as well!
