  Turn old t-shirts into a beautifully upcycled creation!


  In this fun, hands-on workshop, you'll learn how to transform recycled fabric
  into a unique braided rug using the 4-strand braid method—no prior experience
  needed. Waste Knot Orlando will guide you step-by-step, and all materials will
  be provided to get you started.


  Feel free to bring along any old t-shirts (your own or thrifted treasures) if
  you have favorite colors or patterns you'd like to include in your creation.
  Whether you're feeling crafty, looking to reduce waste, or just want a chill
  afternoon with a friendly bunch, this workshop is a great way to make
  something beautiful and useful from what might otherwise be thrown away.
