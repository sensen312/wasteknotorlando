description: >
  Turn old t-shirts into a beautiful rugs, perfect for the kitchen or bathroom. 


  In this fun workshop, you'll learn how to transform recycled fabric into a
  unique braided rug using the 4-strand braid method—no prior experience needed.
  Sarah from Waste Knot Orlando will guide you step-by-step, and t-shirt yarn
  will be provided. Please bring your own scissors. 


  Also, feel free to bring along any old t-shirts if you have favorite colors
  you'd like to include in your creation. 


  The Ladies Homestead Gathering meets the 4th Thursday each month from
  7:00-8:30 pm at Windsor in Celebration (1370 Celebration Blvd). The meetings
  are open to the public.
